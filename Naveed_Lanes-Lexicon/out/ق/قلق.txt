قَلَقَ
, (M, Msb, TA,) inf. n. قَلَقٌ, (S, M,
Msb, K,) It was, or became, unsettled, unsteady,
unfixed, loose, mobile, unquiet, or restless; it
did not settle, become fixed or motionless or quiet
or at rest, or it did not rest or remain or continue,
in its place. (TA.) He, or it, became disquieted,
disturbed, agitated, flurried, or in a state of
unrest or commotion; syn. اِنْزَعَجَ, (S, K, TA,)
and اِضْطَرَبَ. (M, Msb.) ―
-b2-  قَلِقَ إِلَيْهِ, or الى
لِقَائِهِ, see جَنَبَ.
