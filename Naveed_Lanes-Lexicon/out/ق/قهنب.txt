قَهَنَّبٌ
Tall, and having a curving back, the
upper part bending forward over the breast, or
a bent and humped back, or a projecting breast
and hollow back; accord. to the different explanations of أَجْنَأُ: or tall; (K;) i. e., in an absolute sense; (TA;) as also ↓ قَهْنَبَانٌ. (K.) But
AHei and others assert that the ن is augmentative. (MF.)
مُقَهْنِبٌ
Always remaining at the water. (K.)
