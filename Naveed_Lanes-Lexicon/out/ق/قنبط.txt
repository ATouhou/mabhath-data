قنبطقُنَّبِيطٌ [Cauliflower;] the thickest species of
كُرُنْب; (K;) called in the dial. of Egypt قَرْنَبِيطٌ;
mentioned in the S in art. قبط; [but only as a
thing well known;] the author of the S regarding
the ن as augmentative: (TA:) it renders the
breath stinking; and causes a thickness, or grossness, [app. of the humours;] and she who uses its
seed in the manner of a suppository in the vagina
will not become pregnant: (K:) so say the physicians. (TA.)
