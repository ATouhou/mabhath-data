قولنجقُوْلَنْجٌ, (Msb, * K,) with fet-h to the ل, (Msb,)
and sometimes قَوْلِنْجٌ, with kesr to the ل, or thus
and قُولِنْجٌ, i. e. with kesr to the ل and with fet-h
and damm to the ق, (K,) a foreign word, (TA,)
[from the Greek kwliko\s The colic;] a certain
painful intestinal disease, in which the egress of
the feces and wind is attended with difficulty;
(K;) a violent griping in the intestine called the
colon. (Msb.)
