قمد1
قَمِدَ, aor.
قَمَدَ, (TA,) inf. n. قَمَدٌ, (K,) He, or
it, was, or became, tall, or long: or he was, or
became, large and long in the neck. (K, TA.)

hardy: (L:) or gross, thick, coarse, or rude,
