قهقبقَهْقَبٌ
Big, bulky, or large in body; as also
قَهْقَمٌ: (Seer, L:) or the same as an epithet
applied to a camel: (TA:) or big, bulky, or
large in body, and advanced in years; as also
قَهْقَبٌّ, (K,) and قَرْهَبٌ. (TA.) ―
-b2-  Tall, or long,
and having a capacious belly. (K.) ―
-b3-  Hard,
and strong, or robust. (M.) ―
-b4-  I. q.
بَاذِ نْجَانٌ;
(IAar, K:) as also كَهْكَبٌ. (TA.)
