مَقِيلٌ
A resting-place; syn. مُسْتَقَرٌّ: hence, مَقِيلُ
الحُبِّ [the resting-place of love] and مَقِيلُ الغَيْظِ
[the resting-place of wrath], applied by El-Mutanebbee to the heart. (W, i. 112.) See an ex.
(mistranslated) in De Sacy's Ar. Gr., sec. ed.,
ii. 165: the same, with a var., in Ibn-Akeel
p. 210.
