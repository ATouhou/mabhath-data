قِرْنٌ
One who opposes, or contends with,
another, in science, or in fight, &c.; (Msb;) an
opponent; a competitor; an adversary; an antagonist: or one's equal, or match, in courage,
(S, K,) or generally, one's equal, match, or
fellow. (K.)
قَرْنٌ
One's equal in age; syn. لِدَةٌ, (K,) or تِرْبٌ:
with fet-h when relating to age, and with kesr when
relating to fighting and the like. (Har, pp. 572,64.)
