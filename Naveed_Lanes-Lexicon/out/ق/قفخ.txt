قفخ1
قَفَخَهُ, (S, L,) aor.
قَفَخَ, (L,) inf. n. قَفْخٌ and
قِفَاخٌ, (S, L, K,) He struck him, or it: but the
striking termed قفخ is only on the head, or on
something hollow, (S, L, K,) or on something hard
[evidently a mistake for moist, or soft]; (L;)
i. q. فَقَخَهُ, (K,) and used by the people of ElYemen in the sense of صَقَعَهُ: (L:) he struck a
person's head with a staff: (L:) he struck a
person on the head with a staff. (As.) He broke
a person's head: he broke a thing across: he
broke the substance called عَرْمَض upon the surface
of water. (L.)
