قدوقُدْوَةٌ
and قِدْوَةٌ (S, Msb, K,) and قَدْوَةٌ (K,)
A pattern; an exemplar; an example; an object
of imitation; one who is, or is to be, imitated.
(S, Msb, K, TA.) See إِسْوَةٌ.
قِدْيَهٌ
: see فِدْيَهٌ.
قَادِيَةٌ
The first that come to one, or come upon
one, of a company of men. (TA in art. طحم.)
