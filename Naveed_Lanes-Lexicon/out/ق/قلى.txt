He roasted in a
frying-pan (MA, KL) flesh-meat (MA) or anything: (KL:) and شَوَى signifies the same.

[i. e. he parched, or roasted, the wheat with the
مقلى]. (Mgh.) The aor. is يَقْلِى and يَقْلُو, and
the inf. n. قَلْىٌ (MA, Mgh) and قَلْوٌ. (Mgh.)

Potash; as is shown by the explanations
in the S, K, and TA. Hence our term “ alkali. ”
See حُرْضٌ.
