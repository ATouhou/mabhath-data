عيق1
عَيْقٌ [as inf. n. of عَاقَ having يَعِيقُ for its
aor.] is syn. with عَوْقٌ [as inf. n. of عَاقَ having
يَعُوقُ for its aor.], (O, K, TA,) signifying The
act of hindering, preventing, impeding, withholding, or turning back or away. (TA.)
2
عيّق, inf. n. تَعْيِيقٌ, He raised his voice,
called or called out, cried or cried out, or vociferated. (O, K.)
عَيْقٌ A share of water. (L, K.)
عِيقِ (in the CK عِيقُ) A cry used in chiding.
(Ibn-'Abbád, O, K.)
عَيْقَةٌ The shore, and side, of the sea: (A'Obeyd,
S, O, K:) pl. عَيْقَاتٌ. (TA.) ―
-b2-  And an open,
or a wide, space of ground, in front, or extending
from the sides, of a house: or a yard, or court.
(TA.)
