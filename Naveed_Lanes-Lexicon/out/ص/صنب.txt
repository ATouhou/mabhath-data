صنبصِنَابٌ A sauce made of mustard (S, M, A, K)
and raisins. (S, A, K.)
-A2-  And Long in the back
and belly; as also ↓ صِنَابَةٌ: (IAar, O, K:) and so
each with س. (IAar, O.)
صِنَابَةٌ: see what immediately precedes.
صِنَابِىٌّ, applied to a horse (M, A, TA) or
similar beast, (M, TA,) or to a hackney, (TA,)
and to a camel, (M, TA,) Of a colour between
redness and yellowness, (M, A, K, TA,) with
abundance of hair, and of fur: (TA:) or of a
bay, or dark bay, or brown, colour; syn. كُمَيْتٌ:
(S, O, K:) or of a sorrel colour; syn. أَشْقَرُ:
(K:) or of this last colour having some white
hairs intermixed therewith: (S, O, TA:) so
called because his colour resembles the sauce
termed صِنَاب: (TA:) صِنَابِىٌّ being a rel. noun
from صِنَابٌ. (S, O, TA.)
مِصْنَبٌ Addicted to, or fond of, eating the
sauce termed صِنَاب. (IAar, O, K.)
