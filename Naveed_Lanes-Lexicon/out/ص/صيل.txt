صيل1
صَالَ, aor. يَصِيلُ, i. q. صال having for its aor.
يَصُولُ, (Ibn-'Abbád, O, K,) i. e. He (a man)
leaped or sprang [&c.]. (Ibn-'Abbád, O.)
-A2-
صِيلَ لَهُمْ كَذَا, (S and K in art. صول,) or لَهُ, (Ibn-
'Abbád and O and K in the present art.,) Such a
thing was appointed, or ordained, or prepared,
for them, or for him. (Ibn-'Abbád, S, O, K.)
This is its proper place. (TA.)
صِيلَةٌ The knot of the عَذَبَة [i. e. of the end,
or of the suspensory thong, of a whip]. (S and
K in art. صول, and O in the present art.)
صِيَالٌ and صِيَالَةٌ: see art. صول.
Quasi صيم
صُيَّمٌ and صِيَّمٌ and صُيَّامٌ and صَيَامَى pls. of صَائِمٌ,
expl. in art. صوم.
