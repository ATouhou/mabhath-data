صَفَا, (S, M, Msb,) aor. يَصْفُو, (S, Msb,)
inf. n. صَفَآءٌ (S, M, Msb, K *) and صُفُوٌّ (M, Msb,
K *) and صَفْوٌ (K, * TK) and صَفْوَةٌٰ and صِفْوَةٌ,
(MA,) said of wine, or beverage, (S,) or of water,
(TK,) or of a thing, (M,) It was, or became,
clear, limpid, or pure; contr. of كَدِرَ; (S, M,
K; *) or free from الكَدَر [i. e. turbidness, thickness,
or muddiness]; (Msb;) or free from admixture.
(Er-Rághib, TA.) And, said of the air, or
atmosphere, It was, or became, cloudless; free
from any particle of cloud. (M, K.) [And it is
also said, tropically, of life; and of the mind, or
heart; and of love, or affection; &c.] ―

or portion, [of the contents] of the cooking-pot.
