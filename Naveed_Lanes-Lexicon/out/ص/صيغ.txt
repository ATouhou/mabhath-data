صيغ2
صيّغ طَعَامَهُ, (ISh, O, K,) inf. n. تَصْيِيغٌ (K,)
He soaked his food in sauce, or seasoning: (ISh,
O, K:) and صيّغهُ بِالسَّمْنِ He soaked it with
clarified butter. (ISh, O.) [صَبَغَهُ has a similar
meaning, and is better known.]
صِيغَةٌ and other words in which ى is substituted
for و, see in art. صوغ.
