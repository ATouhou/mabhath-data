صعو1
صَعَا, aor.
صَعَوَ, He, or it, was slender; and
was small: (IAar, K:) from صَعْوٌ, here following.
(TA.)
صَعْوٌ A small عُصْفُور [or bird of the sparrowkind], (K, TA,) red in the head; (TA;) fem.
with ة: (K:) or small عَصَافِير [or birds of the
sparrow-kind], the heads of which are red; n. un.
with ة: (Msb:) or صَعْوَةٌ signifies a certain bird;
and its pl. is صَعْوٌ and صِعَآءٌ: (S:) or the pl. (of
صَعْوَةٌ, Msb) is صِعَآءٌ (Msb, K) and صَعَوَاتٌ, (K, in
the CK صعْواتٌ,) and the pl. of صَعْوٌ is أَصْعَآءٌ:
some say that صَعْوٌ is originally وَصْعٌ. (TA.) ―
-b2-
Hence, (TA,) نَاقَةٌ صَعْوَةٌ A she-camel small in the
head. (K.)
-A2-  And the pl. أَصْعَآءٌ signifies أُصُولٌ
[pl. of أَصْلٌ, q. v.]. (TA.)
