صِنَّارٌ, (K,) or ↓ صِنَّارَةٌ, (S, O, M,) or the latter
is not allowable, (TA,) The head of a spindle;
(S, O, K;) i. e. (S) the crooked, (S,) or slender,
(M,) or slender and crooked, (TA,) piece of iron
(S, M, TA) that is in the head of the spindle:
(M, TA:) or, accord. to Lth, the latter signifies a
woman's spindle; and is a foreign word introduced into the Arabic language. (TA.)
