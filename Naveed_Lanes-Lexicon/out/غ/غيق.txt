غيقغَاقٌ A certain aquatic bird; as also ↓ غَاقَةٌ.
(Lth, O, K.) ―
-b2-  And The crow, or raven:
(ISd, K:) so sometimes called because of his
cry. (ISd, TA.)
-A2-  غَاقِ is a word imitative of
The cry [i. e. caw] of the crow, or raven: when
indeterminate, [meaning a caw,] it is with tenween, (IJ, S, O, K,) i. e. غَاقٍ. (IJ, TA.)
غَاقَةٌ: see the preceding paragraph.
