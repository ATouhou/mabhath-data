غفص3
غافصهُ, (S, A, Msb, K,) inf. n. مُغَافَصَةٌ and
غِفَاصٌ, (TA,) He came upon him suddenly, or
unexpectedly; (A, Msb, K;) he took him at unawares, (S, Msb, K,) and did an evil action to
him. (TA.) ―
-b2-  Hence the saying, (Msb,) أَخَذْتُهُ
مُغَافَصَةً (A, Msb, TA) I took it (a thing, Msb)
striving to overcome. (Msb, TA.)
غَافِصَةٌ A sudden, or an unexpected, calamity,
of time, or fortune: (Sgh, * K, * TK:) pl. غَوَافِصُ.
(A.) You say, وَقَاكَ اللّٰهُ غَوَافِصَ الدَّهْرِ [May God
preserve thee from the sudden, or unexpected,
calamities of time, or fortune]. (A.)
