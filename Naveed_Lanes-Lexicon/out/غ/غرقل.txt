غرقلQ. 1
غَرْقَلَتِ البَيْضَةُ The egg became rotten;
(S, O;) the interior of the egg became corrupt:
and thus غَرْقَلَ signifies said of the بِطِّيخ [i. e.
melon, or water-melon]: (K:) or, said of the
بطّيخ, it became hard. (O.)
-A2-  And غَرْقَلَ He
poured water upon his head at once; (IAar, O,
K, TA;) inf. n. غَرْقَلَةٌ. (TA.)
غِرْقِلٌ The white of an egg; as also ↓ غِرْقِيلٌ.
(Az, TA.)
غِرْقِيلٌ: see what next precedes.
