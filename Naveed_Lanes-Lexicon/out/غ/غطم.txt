غطمغِطَمٌّ A great sea, (S, K, TA,) abundant in
water; (S, TA;) and ↓ غِطْيَمٌّ, (K, TA,) like
قِرْشَبُّ, (TA,) [in the CK غَطِيْم,] signifies the
same; as also ↓ غَطَمْطَمٌ: (K, TA:) one says بَحْرٌ
غِطَمٌّ. (S.) ―
-b2-  And (assumed tropical:)  A man large (S, K) in disposition (S) or dispositions; (K;) [i. e.] liberal,
or bountiful: (TA:) one says رَجُلٌ غِطَمٌّ. (S.)
―
-b3-  And A numerous company, or collective body:
(K, TA:) one says جَمْعٌ غِطَمٌّ. (S, TA.) And
↓ عَدَدٌ غِطْيَمٌّ, like قِرْشَبٌّ, A large number. (TA.)
[See also غُطَامِطٌ, in art. غطمط.]
غِطْيَمٌّ: see the preceding paragraph, in two
places.
غَطَمُطَمٌ: see the first paragraph.
غَيْطَمٌّ Thick milk. (K, TA.)
