They leagued together, or collected themselves. together, and aided one another, against him. (T.)
أَلْبٌ (T, S, Msb) and ↓ إِلْبٌ (S, Msb) Persons,

people: (T:) and ↓ أَلْبٌ أَلُوبٌ a great assembly

-b2-  Also A people, or

or hostility: (Lth, T, M, K:) like وَعْلٌ وَاحِدٌ
and صَدْعٌ وَاحِدٌ and ضِلَعٌ وَاحِدٌ. (T, TA.)
إِلْبٌ: see أَلْبٌ, in two places.
