افجن He (a man, TA) kept constantly to
the eating of فَيْجَن [i. e. rue]. (K, TA.)
فَيْجَنٌ [*ph/ganon; i. e. rue;] i. q. سَذَابٌ; (S, K;)
as also ↓ فيجين: (T in art. خفت, and TA in art.
ختف:) and so فَيْجَلٌ: (TA:) IDrd [rightly]
says, “I do not think it to be a genuine Arabic
word. ” (TA.)
فيجين: see the next preceding paragraph.
