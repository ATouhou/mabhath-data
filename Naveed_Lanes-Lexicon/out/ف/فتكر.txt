فتكرفِتْكِرٌ and فِتَكْرٌ: see what follows.
فِتَكْرِين and فُتَكْرِين (S, O, K) and فَتَكْرِين and
فِتْكَرِين and ↓ فِتْكِرٌ (O, K) and ↓ فِتَكْرٌ (K) A calamity, or misfortune; (O, K;) and [in the CK
“ or ”] a wonderful, and great, or formidable,
affair, or event: (K:) or the ن is the characteristic of the pl.; and one says, لَقِيتُ مِنْهُ الفِتَكْرِينَ
and الفُتَكْرِينَ, meaning [I experienced from him,
or it,] difficulties, or distresses, and calamities, or
misfortunes. (S.)
