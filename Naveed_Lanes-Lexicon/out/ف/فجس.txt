فجس1
فَجَسَ, aor.
فَجُسَ, (S, TA,) inf. n. فَجْسٌ, (S, O,
K, TA,) He behaved proudly, or magnified himself; (S, O, K, TA;) as also ↓ تفجّس; (S, * K;)
or this latter signifies he magnified, or exalted,
himself, (O, TA,) and boasted: (O, * TA:) and
فَجْزٌ signifies the same as فَجْسٌ, i. e. the “ behaving proudly, or magnifying oneself. ” (TA.)
―
-b2-  And He overcame, or subdued, or oppressed.
(Ibn-'Abbád, O, K: but only the inf. n. of the
verb in this and the following sense is mentioned.)
―
-b3-  He did an unprecedented act, and only one of
an evil kind. (Ibn-'Abbád, O, K, TA.)
4
افجس He (a man) boasted vainly, or falsely.
(IAar, O, K.)
