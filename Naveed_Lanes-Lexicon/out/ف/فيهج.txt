فيهجفَيْهَجٌ A wine-measure: (S, O, K:) a Pers. word
arabicized [and therefore all its letters are regarded
as radical]. (S.) ―
-b2-  And A strainer (O, K, TA)
for wine. (TA.) ―
-b3-  And (sometimes, S, O) a
name for Wine: (S, K:) or clear wine: (O, TA:)
or an epithet applied to wine [app. signifying
clear]: (TA:) or wine such as is مختلق [app. a
mistranscription for مُخَلَّق, a word now used as
signifying aromatized]; like قِنْدِيدٌ [q. v.] and أُمٌّ
زَنْبَقٍ. (IAmb, TA.)
