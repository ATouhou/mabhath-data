روف1
رَافَ, (IDrd, K,) aor. يَرَافُ, (K,) or يَرُوفُ,
inf. n. رَوْفٌ, (IDrd,) is a dial. var. of رَأَفَ, (IDrd,
K,) aor. يَرْأَفُ: (K:) or, as some say, (IDrd,)
رَوْفٌ signifies The being still; and is not from
الرَّأْفَةُ: (IDrd, K:) [ISd says,] رَافَ, inf. n. رَوْفٌ,
signifies سَكَنَ [he, or it, was, or became, still,
&c.] and رَأَفَ [q. v.] is a dial. var. thereof [signifying thus]; and is not from رَؤُوفٌ syn. with
رَحِيمٌ. (M.)
3
راوف: see رَأَفَ.
رَافٌ Wine; a dial. var. of رَأْفٌ. (TA.)
رَوْفَةٌ Mercy, or compassion: (O, K:) so accord. to IAar. (O.)
