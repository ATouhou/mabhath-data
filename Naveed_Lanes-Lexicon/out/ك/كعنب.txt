كعنبكَعْنَبٌ Short: (K:) an epithet applied to a
man. (Az.)
-A2-  الكَعْنَبُ and الكُعَانِبُ The lion.
(K.)
-A3-  كَعَانِبُ Knobs, or protuberances, (عُجَرٌ,)
of the head. (K.) كَعْنَبٌ A man having knobs,
or protuberances, (عُجَرٌ,) on his head. (K.)
تَيْسٌ مُكَعْنَبُ القَرْنِ A he-goat having the horn
curved so as to resemble a ring: (K:) like
مُشَعْنَب, q. v. (TA.)
