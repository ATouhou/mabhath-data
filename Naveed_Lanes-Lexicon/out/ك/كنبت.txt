كنبتQ. 2
تَكَنْبَتَ He (a man) became contracted
[in disposition; or niggardly, or stingy]. (L, as
from IDrd.) But see art. كنبث. (TA.)
كُنْبُبٌ, (or this should be كُنْثُبٌ, TA) A
hard, strong, robust, man. (L.) But see كُنْبُثٌ.
(TA.) ―
-b2-  Also, and كُنَابِتٌ, A man contracted
[in disposition]; niggardly, or stingy. (L.)
But see art. كنبث. (TA.)
