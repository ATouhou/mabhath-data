كنبثQ. 1
كَنْبَثَ and ↓ تَكَنْبَثَ He became hard and
strong: (L:) he became contracted; syn. تَقَبَّضَ:
(K:) [app. in disposition; see كُنْبُثٌ: or in
make; the second verb being also expl. in the L,
with reference to a man, by the words تَدَاخَلَ
بَعْضُهُ فِى بَعْضٍ].
Q. 2
تَكَنْبَثَ see Q. 1.
كُنْبُثٌ and ↓ كُنْبُوثٌ and ↓ كُنَابثٌِ Hard (L, K)
and strong. (L.) [Epithets applied to a man.]
―
-b2-  Also, all the three words, Contracted [in
disposition], and niggardly, or stingy. (K.) ―
-b3-
Mentioned before in art. كبث; and like كُلْبُثٌ.
(TA.) ―
-b4-  [Accord. to the L, these epithets seem
also to apply to a man Contracted in make: the
first and third being expl. by the words تَدَاخَلَ
بَعْضُهُ فِى بَعْضٍ.]
كُنْبُوثٌ: see كُنْبُثٌ.
كُنَابثٌ: see كُنْبُثٌ and art. كنثب.
