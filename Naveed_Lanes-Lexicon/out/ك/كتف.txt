كتفكَتِفٌ
[The shoulder-blade;] a wide bone
behind the shoulder-joint. (Mgh.) ―
-b2-  [Hence,
The shoulder itself.] See طُرَّةٌ and مُؤُرَّبٌ.
كُِتْفاَنٌ
, as an epithet applied to the locust, see
in TA, voce مُسَيَّحٌ. See also جَرَادٌ.
كِتَافٌ
of a زَبِيل: see حَتِىٌّ.
كَتِيفَةٌ
i. q.
ضَبَّةٌ
A broad piece of iron. A
poet speaks of a wooden vessel of which a
fracture is mended with a كتيفة. (S.)
