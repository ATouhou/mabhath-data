كُفَّةٌ
The selvage, i. e. border, or side, of a
garment or piece of cloth, (S, Msb,) that has no
هُدْب [or end of unwoven threads]: (S, voce طُرَّةٌ:)
or what surrounds the skirt of a shirt: or whatever is oblong; as the
حَاشِيَة
of a garment or
piece of cloth, and of sand: and the edge of a
thing. (K.)
كِفَّةٌ
The bezel, or collet, i. e. the part in
which the stone is set, of a signet-ring. (TA in
art. ركب.) See كُرْسِىٌّ. ―
-b2-  See also طَرْقٌ. ―
