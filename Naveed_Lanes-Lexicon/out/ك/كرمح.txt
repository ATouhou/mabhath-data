كرمحQ. 1
كَرْمَحَ, inf. n. كَرْمَحَةٌ, i. q. كَرْتَحَ, (S,
art. كردح, and CK, and a MS copy of the
K,) He (a short man) ran with short steps, and
quickly: (S, ubi supra:) or i. q. كَرْبَحَ, the ب
being changed into م, (TA,) he ran at a slower
pace than that termed كَرْدَمَةٌ. (L, TA.) ―
-b2-
كَرْمَحْنَا فِى آثَارِ القَوْمِ We ran heavily in the
footsteps of, or after, the people, (AA, S, ubi
supra, L.)
