كعبركُعْبُرَةٌ
and ↓ كُعْبُورَةٌ
Anything compact (K,
TA) and round: (TA:) and the latter, i. q.
عُقْدَةٌ
[meaning a knot, or a knob]: (TA:) [pl. of the
latter كَعَابِيرُ: see خِرْوٌ: see also أَخْرَمُ]. ―
-b2-  Also
The seed-vessel [i. e. siliqua, or pod,] of the radish
and some other plants. (IAar, TA voce إِسْحَارٌّ.)
كُعْبُوْرَةٌ
