زردمQ. 1
زَرْدَمَهُ, (S, K,) inf. n. زَرْدَمَةٌ, (TA,) He
squeezed his throat: (S, K:) or he throttled him;
or strangled him: (K:) as also زَرْدَبَهُ. (TA.) ―
-b2-
And He swallowed it. (K.)
زَرْدَمَةٌ inf. n. of the verb above. (TA.)
-A2-  Also
The place of swallowing: (S, K:) or the غَلْصَمَة
[or epiglottis]: (K:) or, as some say, it is [the
part] beneath the حُلْقُوم [or windpipe: or, app.,
the part beneath the head of the حلقوم]: and in
it, or upon it, is set the tongue: some say that the
word is Pers. [in origin; app. holding it to be
arabicized from the Pers. زَرْدَمَنْ, which signifies
the “ windpipe ”]. (TA.)
