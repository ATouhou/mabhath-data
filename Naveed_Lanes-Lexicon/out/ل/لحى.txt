لَحْىٌ [not لَحًى] The jaw-bone; (Mgh, Msb;)
either upper or lower: (Msb:) and, in a man,
(Msb,) the part on which the beard grows. (S,
Msb, K.) ―
-b2-  And sometimes Either lateral portion of the lower jaw: see ذَقَنٌ, and الفَنِيكُ.
لِحْيَةُ التَّيْسِ: see ثِيلٌ. ―
-b2-  لِحْيَةٌ is sometimes,
by a synecdoche, put for the whole person: see
a verse cited voce خُنْثٌ.
مَسْحُ اللُِّحَى: see عَقَّ بِالسَّهْمِ.
لِحَآءٌ Bark; and in particular the bark of a
plant, or of a tree, of which ropes &c. are made.
―
-b2-  The لِحَآء of the date is [its Pulp, pulpy
pericarp, or flesh; i. e.] the part that clothes the
نَوَاة [or stone]. (TA.) ―
-b3-  لِحَآءُ العِنَبِ [The skin
of grapes]. (TA in art. شحم.)
