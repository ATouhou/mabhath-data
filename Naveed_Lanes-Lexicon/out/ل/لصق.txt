لَاصَقَهُ He associated with him.
لَصِيقٌ An associate; an adherent.
مُلْصَقٌ i. q. دَعِىٌّ (TA;) as also ↓ مُلَصَّقٌ (TA in
art. لسق) and مُلَسَّقٌ: (K, and TA in that art.:)
or [a consociated alien;] one residing among a
tribe of which he is not a member by lineage.
(TA.)
مُلَصَّقٌ: see مُلْصَقٌ.
