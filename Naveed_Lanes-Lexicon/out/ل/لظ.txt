He kept, or clave, or adhered, (T, S, M, K,) to

it: (T, S, M:) he applied himself to it perseveringly, assiduously, constantly, or incessantly;
(A'Obeyd, * S, * M, TA;) as also الظّ عَلَيْهِ: (M:)
and بِهِ ↓ لَظَّ, (IDrd, M, TA,) [aor., accord. to

لَظِ3َ,] inf. n. لَظٌّ and ↓ لَظِيظٌ, (K, TA,)
or the latter is a subst. from الظّ به; (M, TA;)
in the former sense, (K,) and in the latter.
(M, K.) You say, الظّ بِالكَلِمَةِ He kept to the
expression. (M.) And hence, أَلِظُّوا فِى الدُّعَآءِ
بِيَا ذَا الجَلَالِ وَالإِكْرَامِ Keep ye in prayer to
