لختلَخْتٌ Great, or big, in body: (Lth, Sgh, and
some copies of the K:) or great, or big, and
corpulent. (So in the other copies of the K.) ―
-b2-
A woman in whom the division between the vagina
and the rectum has been broken through; syn.
مُفُضَاةٌ. (K.) ―
-b3-  حَرٌّ سَخْتٌ لَخْتٌ Vehement, or
intense, heat. (Lth, K.) [See also سَخْتٌ: and
see لَحْتٌ.] Thought by ISd to be arabicized.
(TA.)
