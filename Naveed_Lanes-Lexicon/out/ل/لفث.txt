لفث10
استلفث مَا عِنْدَهُ He elicited, and exhausted,
(إِسْتَنْبَطَ وَاسْتَقْصَى,) what [information, &c.] he
had, or possessed. (K.) ―
-b2-  استلفث الرِّعْىَ He
consumed the whole of the pasture, leaving nothing of it. (K, TA.) ―
-b3-  استلفث حَاجَتَهُ He
accomplished his want. (K.) ―
-b4-  استلفث الخَبَرَ
He concealed the news. (K.)
أَلْفَثُ Stupid; foolish; of little sense: (K:)
like أَلْفَتُ. (TA.)
