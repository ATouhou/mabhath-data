لوأ1
لَآءَ, aor. يَلُوْءُ, inf. n. لَوْءَةٌ, app., He was
foul, ugly, or deformed, in countenance.] لَوْءَةٌ,
accord. to the K, is the same as سَوْءَةٌ; but this is
probably a mistake for شَوْءَةٌ, The being foul, ugly,
or deformed, in countenance; [which is the signification of لَوَّةٌ]. (TA.) [See art. لوى.] ―
-b2-  هٰذَا
وَاللّٰهِ الشَّوْءَةُ وَاللَّوْءَةُ, and اللَّوَّةُ, [app., This, by
Allah, is foulness and ugliness!] (TA [app. from
the T.])
2
لوّأ He looked malignantly with his eyes or
countenance. (TA: app. from the T.) ―
-b2-  لوّأ اللّٰهُ
بِكَ May God render thee foul, or ugly! (T.)
