-b5-
لَتَأَتْ بِهِ (tropical:)  She (a woman) brought him forth.
(S, K. *) ―
-b6-  لَعَنَ اللّٰهُ أُمًّا لَتَأَتْ بِهِ, and لَكَأَتْ به,
(S,) (tropical:)  God curse the mother that cast him forth
(from her womb) ! a tropical expression, from
casting a stone, or shooting an arrow. (TA.) ―
