ذوف1
ذَافَ, (M, K,) aor. يَذُوفُ, (M,) inf. n. ذَوْفٌ,
He walked with short steps, and in a straddling
manner. (M, K.)
-A2-  And ذُفْتُ is a dial. var. of
ذُفْتُ, signifying I mixed [medicine &c.] (M.)
ذُوفَانٌ [like ذُؤْفَانٌ &c.] Poison: (K:) or poison
made into a confection: or deadly poison: like
ذِيفَانٌ [&c.]. (M.)
