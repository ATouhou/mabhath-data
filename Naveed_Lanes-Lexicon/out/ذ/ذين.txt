ذين1
ذَانَهُ, [aor. يَذِينُ, inf. n., app., ذَيْنٌ,] He
blamed, or found fault with, him, or it; like ذَامَهُ.
(IAar, T.)
ذَيْنٌ, (M, TA,) incorrectly said in the K to be
ذِيْنٌ, with kesr, (TA,) A vice, fault, defect, or
the like, (M, K, TA;) as also ذَانٌ [which belongs
to art. ذون]. (M.)
مُذَانٌ a dial. var. of مُذَالٌ. (M.) [See the
latter in art. ذيل.]
