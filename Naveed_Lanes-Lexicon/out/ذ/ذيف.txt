ذيفذِيفَانٌ and ذَيْفَانٌ (S, M, K) and ذَيَفَانٌ (M, K)
Deadly poison: (S, M, K:) or poison that takes
effect; or that remains fixed, and collects: (M:)
a dial. var. of ذِئْفَانٌ &c., (K,) and ذُوفَانٌ. (M.)
And the second of these words, Death: so in the
saying, سَقَاهُ اللّٰهُ كَأْسَ الذَّيْفَانِ [May God give
him to drink the cup of death]; as mentioned by
Lh. (M.)
