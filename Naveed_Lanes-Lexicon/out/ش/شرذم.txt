شرذمشِرْذِمَةٌ A party, or company, (طَائِفَةٌ,) of men,
or people: (S:) or a small company: (TA:) or a
small number of men, or people: (K:) and so
شِرْدِمَةٌ, with the unpointed د, on the authority of
AA: (IB, TA:) the former occurring in the Kur
xxvi. 54. (TA.) ―
-b2-  A piece, or portion, (S, K,)
of a thing, (S,) of a quince &c.: pl. شَرَاذِمُ and
شَرَاذِيمُ. (K.) ―
-b3-  [Hence,] ثَوْبٌ شَرَاذِمُ, (S,) or
ثِيَابٌ شَرَاذِمُ, (K,) A garment, or garments, old
and worn out, (S, K,) much rent. (K.)
