شمردلشَمَرْدَلٌ A youth, strong and hardy; and in the
like sense applied to a camel: (Lth, TA:) or
tall; as also ↓ شَمَرْدَلِىٌّ: (Ham pp. 728 and 789:)
or a bulky camel: (IAar, TA:) or, of camels &c.,
(S, O, K,) the swift; (S, O;) or youthful, swift,
and goodly in make: (K:) and with ة, a she-camel goodly, beautiful in make. (A 'Obeyd, S,
O, K.)
شَمَرْدَلِىٌّ: see the preceding paragraph.
