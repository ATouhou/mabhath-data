شبتشِبِتٌّ [Anethum graveolens, or dill, of the
common garden-species;] a certain herb, or leguminous plant, well known: (K:) it is said that
سِبِتٌّ is an arabicized word from شِبِتٌّ; but it has
been stated before [in art. سبت, q. v.,] that both these
are arabicized words from شِوِذْ [or شِوِدْ]; and that
سبط [i. e. سِطٌّ] is a dial. var. (TA.) [See also شِبِثٌّ.]
