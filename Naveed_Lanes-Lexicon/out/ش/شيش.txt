شيش4
اشاشت النَّخْلَةُ The palm-tree produced dates
such as are termed شِيش. (O, K.)
شِيشٌ and ↓ شِيشَآءٌ A sort of dates which do not
organize and compact stones; (Fr, O, K;) or, if
they do so, they do not become hard; and when
they dry, they become such as are termed حَشَف,
not sweet: (O, K:) dial. vars. of شِيصٌ and شِيصَآءٌ:
(S:) accord. to AHn, (TA,) of Persian origin.
(O, TA.)
شِيشَآءٌ: see the next preceding paragraph.
