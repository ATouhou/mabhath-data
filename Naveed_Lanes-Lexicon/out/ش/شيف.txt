شيف2
شيّف الدَّوَآءَ [from شِيَافٌ]: see 2 in art. شوف.
شِيفٌ The prickles (شَوْك) that are at the back of
the عَسِيب [or leafless portion, next above the
lowest and thickest part, of the branch] of the
palm-tree: (O, K:) so says AHát: (O:) but
Lth says that the word is [سِيفٌ, q. v.,] with the
unpointed س. (TA.)
شِيَافٌ, originally شِوَافٌ: see art. شوف.
شِيِّفَةٌ and شِيِّفَانٌ: see art. شوف.
