خوّد, (L,) inf. n. تَخْوِيدٌ, (S, L, K,) He (a
camel, L) went quickly; or was quick in his pace
or going: (S, L, K:) he went quickly, and threw
out his legs: or, as some say, he shook, as though
he were convulsed: and in like manner, a male
ostrich: and sometimes it is said of a man, signifying as first explained above. (L.) ―
