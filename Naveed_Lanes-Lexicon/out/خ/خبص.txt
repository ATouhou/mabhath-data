-b2-  A
kind of food, (Msb, TA,) sweet, (TA,) well known,
(S, Msb, TA,) made of dates and clarified butter,
(A, K, TA,) mixed together: (TA:) [Golius

مِخْبَصَةٌ A spoon, or thing like a spoon, with
