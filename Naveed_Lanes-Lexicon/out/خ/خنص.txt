خِنَوْصٌ The young of the swine: (Ibn-'Abbád,
S, K:) and the young, or little, of anything:
(K:) pl. خَنَانِيصُ. (S, K.) [See also خِنَّوْسٌ.]
خِنَّوْصَةٌ The young of the بَبْر, q. v.; (K;) as
also ↓ خِنْصِيصٌ. (Sgh, K.) ―
-b2-  Also A palm-tree
which does not rise beyond reach of the hand.
(Ibn-'Abbád, K.)
