خشو1
خَشَتِ النَّخْلَةُ, aor. تَخْشُو, (JK, S, K,) inf. n.
َشْوٌ, (JK, TA,) The palm-tree bore dates such
as are termed خَشْو, i. e. حَشَف: (JK, S, K:) so
says El-Umawee, (S,) or IAar. (TA.)
خَشًا Black wheat. (IAar, K, * TA.)
خَشْوٌ Dates such as are termed حَشَف: (JK,
S, K:) so says El-Umawee, (S,) or IAar, who
adds, i. e., of which the lower portion has become
bad and rotten, while in its place: he says that
it is of the dial. of Belhárith Ibn-Kaab. (TA.)
