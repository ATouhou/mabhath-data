خيشخَيْشٌ Garments, or pieces of cloth, of the worst
of flax: (S:) or garments, or pieces of cloth, of
thin texture, and of coarse threads, made of the
hards, or hurds, of flax, (K, * TA,) and of the
worst thereof: (TA:) or of the coarsest of [the
stuff called] عصب [i. e. عَصْب, q. v., in the copies
of the K in my hands incorrectly written عَصَب]:
(Lth, K:) or coarse flax: (Mgh:) or a cloth of
coarse flax. (Har p. 544.) ―
-b2-  [Hence,] (assumed tropical:)  A low,
vile, or mean, man. (K.)
خَيْشِىٌّ and ↓ خَيَّاشٌ [A weaver, or seller, of
خَيْش.The former mentioned in the K, and the
latter in the TA, as surnames of men.]
خَيَّاشٌ: see what next precedes.
