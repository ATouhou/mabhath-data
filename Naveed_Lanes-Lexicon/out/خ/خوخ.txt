n. un., (S,) ↓ خَوْخَةٌ. (S, K.)

the light (S, A, K) to a house, or chamber. (K.)

-b2-  A passage (مُخْتَرَقٌ) between any two houses,
not having a door, or gate: (K:) of the dial. of
El-Hijáz; (TA;) [and of Egypt, where it is applied to a lane leading from one street or quarter
to another: coll. gen. n. ↓ خَوْخٌ:] accord. to
some, a passage (مُخْتَرَقٌ) between any two things:

chambers, with a door affixed to it. (L.) ―
