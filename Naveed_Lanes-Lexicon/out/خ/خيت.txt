خيت1
خَاتَ, aor. يَخِيتُ, (IAar,) inf. n. خَيْتٌ and
خُيُوتٌ, (IAar, K,) and inf. n. of un. خَيْتَةٌ, (IAar,)
He made a sound, syn. صَوَّتَ; (IAar, K;*) [app.
with his wings, in pouncing down, or making a
stoop; see 1 in art. خوت;] said of a bird. (IAar.)
-A2-  خَاتَهُ مَالَهُ, aor. يَخِيتُهُ: see 1 in art. خوت.
