Dried and other fruits (such as nuts,
almonds, raisins, dried figs, dried dates, &c.),
[and comfits:] the fruit [that is an accompaniment] of wine; (MA in explanation of نُقْلٌ
[which is more common than نَقْلٌ];) fruit that
is eaten with wine. (KL in explanation of
نُِقْلٌ.)

المَنْقُولُ
[Discerned knowledge; opposed to
مَعْقُولُ]: under this term are comprised the
sciences of أُصُولُ الدِّينِ (also called عِلْمُ الكام).
الحَدِيثُ, and الفِقْهُ: all the other sciences are
comprised under the term المَعْقُولُ; (IbrD;) i. e.
intellectual, or perceived by the intellect; and
excogitated.
