بوس1
بَاسَهُ, aor. يَبُوسُهُ, (S,) inf. n. بَوْسٌ, a Persian
word, arabicized, (S, A, K,) He kissed him. (S,
A, K.) You say also, بَاسَ لَهُ الأَرْضَ He kissed
the ground to him. (A, TA.)
مَبُوسٌ Kissed: you say, اليَوْمَ بِسَاطُكَ مَبُوسٌ وَ غَدًا
أَنْتَ مَحْبُوسٌ [To-day thy carpet is kissed, and
to-morrow thou art imprisoned]. (A.)
