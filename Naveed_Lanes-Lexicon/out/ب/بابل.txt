بابلبَابِلِىٌّ Of, or belonging to, or relating to, بَابِل
[i. e. Babel], a place [well known] in El-'Irák:
it is an epithet applied to enchantment, [which is
said to have been there taught by two fallen
angels, Hároot and Mároot, (see the Kur ii. 96,)]
and to wine. (S, K, TA.) ―
-b2-  And hence, (TA,)
Poison: [and, accord to the CK, wine;] as also
↓ بَابِلِيَّةٌ. (K, TA.) ―
-b3-  In the original language
of the place above mentioned, البَابِلِىُّ is a name of
المُشْتَرِى [The planet Jupiter]. (TA.)
بَابِليَّةٌ: see above.
