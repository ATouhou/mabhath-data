برعمQ. 1
بَرْعَمَتِ الشَّجَرَةُ The tree put forth its
بَرَاعِيم [pl. of بُرْعُومٌ], (S,) or its بُرْعُمَة. (M, K.)
بُرْعُمٌ: see what next follows.
بُرْعُمَةٌ: see what next follows.
بُرْعُومٌ and ↓ بُرْعُمٌ (S, K) and ↓ بُرْعُومَةٌ and ↓ بُرْعُمَةٌ
(K) The calyx of the fruit, or produce, of a tree:
(K:) and blossoms, or white blossoms, syn. نَوْرٌ,
(K TA,) before they open: (TA:) or flowers,
(S, and Msb in explanation of the first word in
art. زهر,) or the flower of a tree, (K,) before the
opening thereof: (S, Msb ubi suprà, K:) pl.
بَرَاعِيمُ. (S, TA.) ―
-b2-  بَرَاعِيمُ also signifies The heads,
or tops; or round, high, slender tops; or peaks;
(شَمَارِيخ;) of mountains: (AZ, K:) sing. ↓ بُرْعُومَةٌ.
(AZ, TA.)
بُرْعُومَةٌ: see بُرْعُومٌ, in two places.
