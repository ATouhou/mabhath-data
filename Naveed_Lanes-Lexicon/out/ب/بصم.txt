بصمبُصْمٌ The space that is between the extremity of
the little finger and that of the third finger [when
they are extended apart]: (S, M, * K:) mentioned
on the authority of AO, (S,) or on that of Aboo-Málik alone. (M.) The عَتَب is the space between
the third finger and the middle finger; the رَتَب,
that between the middle finger and the first
finger; [but see these two words;] the فِتْر, that
between the first finger and the thumb; the شِبْر,
that between the thumb and the little finger; and the
فَوْت, that between every two fingers, in length. (S.)
―
-b2-  ذُو بُصْمٍ Thick, or coarse; applied to a man,
(M, K,) or a garment, or piece of cloth: (K:) or
you say ثَوْبٌ لَهُ بُصْمٌ, meaning a garment, or
piece of cloth, that is dense, or compact; close in
texture. (M.)
