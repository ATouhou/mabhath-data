جرثجِرِّيثٌ A kind of fish, (S, Mgh, K,) well
known, (TA,) also called جِرِّىٌّ and صِلَّوْرٌ, (Mgh,
TA,) resembling a serpent, called in Persian
مَارْ مَاهِى [snake-fish, or eel]; forbidden to be
eaten by the Jews: whether it be lawful to the
Muslims is disputed: (TA:) قِرِّيثٌ is a dial. var.
(S in art. قرث.)
