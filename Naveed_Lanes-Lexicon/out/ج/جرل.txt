جرلجِرْيَالٌ A certain red dye. (As, S, K.) ―
-b2-  The
redness of gold. (S, K.) ―
-b3-  Pure; applied to red
and other colours. (K.) ―
-b4-  Also, (S, K,) and
↓ جِرْيَالَةٌ, (K,) Wine; (S, K;) inferior in goodness to such as is termed سُلَافٌ: (S:) or the
colour of wine. (S, K.) The phrase سَلَبْتُهَا جِرْيَالَهَا,
used by El-Aashà, [lit. I deprived it, namely,
wine, of its colour,] means I drank it red, and
discharged it in urine white. (S.)
جِرْيَالَةٌ: see above.
