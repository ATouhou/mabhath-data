جلهقجُلَاهِقٌ Bullets, syn. بُنْدُقٌ, (S, Msb, K,) or
rounded things, (En-Nadr, TA,) made of clay,
(En-Nadr, Msb, TA,) which one shoots [from a
cross-bow]: (K:) n. un. with ة: (En-Nadr,
Msb, TA:) a Persian word, arabicized; (Msb;)
in Persian جُلَهْ, meaning “ a ball of thread; ”
pl. جُلَهَا; applied also to “ a weaver; ” (S K;)
i. e., جلها is so applied. (TA.) Hence, قَوْسُ
الجُلَاهِقِ [The cross-bow for shooting bullets].
(S, Msb.)
Quasi جلهم
جَلْهَمَةٌ: see art. جله.
