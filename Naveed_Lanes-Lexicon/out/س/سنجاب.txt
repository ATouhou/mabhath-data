سنجابسِنْجَابٌ [a Pers. word, arabicized; in the pre
sent day applied to The squirrel; and particularly
the gray squirrel: and the minever:] a certain
animal, of the length of the jerboa, larger than
the فَأْر [or rat], the fur of which is of the utmost
softness: furred garments are made with its
skin; and the best skins of this animal are the
smooth and gray. (Dmr, TA.)
[
سِنْجَابِىٌّ Gray; of the colour of the سِنْجَاب.]
