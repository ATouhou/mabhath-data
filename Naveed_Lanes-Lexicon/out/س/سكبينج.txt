سكبينجسَكْبِينَجٌ an arabicized word [from the Pers.
سَكْبِينَهٌ]; (O;) [Sagapenum;] a certain medicine,
(O, K,) well-known; (K;) the gum of a certain
tree (O, TA) in Persia, (TA,) [supposed to be
the ferula Persica,] in which is no useful property, but only in its gum: some say that there
is a sort of قِنَّة [or galbanum] that changes from
its original state and becomes سكبينج. (O.)
