سَجمَ الدَّمْعُ, (S, K, JM,) aor.
سَجُمَ, (JM,)
inf. n. سُجُومٌ and سِجَامٌ; (S, K;) and ↓ انسجم;
(S, JM;) The tears flowed: (JM:) or poured
forth; (TA in explanation of the latter verb;)
and so the latter verb said of water. (TA.) ―
