سمهدرسَمَهْدَرٌ Fat, as an epithet, (S, K,) applied to a
boy, in praise of his fleshiness; (Fr, S;) a boy
fat and fleshy. (TA.) ―
-b2-  (tropical:)  The penis, (K,) as
likened to a fat, or fat and fleshy, boy. (TA.) ―
-b3-
Applied to a country, or region, (بَلَدٌ,) Ample,
(S, K,) wide, or far-extending, in its limits: or
in which the sight is perplexed by its levelness.
(TA.) ―
-b4-  A land far-extending; that causes one
to lose his way in it. (K, TA.)
