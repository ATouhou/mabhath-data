سنبقسُنْبُوقٌ A small زَوْرَق [or skiff], (Sgh, K, TA,)
made on the coasts of the sea: a word of the
dial. of the people of all the coasts of the Sea of
El-Yemen: (Sgh, TA:) whether the ن be radical
requires consideration: Sgh says, in the Tekmileh, that the word is of the measure فُنْعُولٌ,
from السَّبْقُ. (TA.)
