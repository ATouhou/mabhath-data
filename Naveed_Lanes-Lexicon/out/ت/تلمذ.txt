تلمذQ. 1
تَلْمَذَ He was, or became, a تِلْمِيذ [or
disciple, &c.], لِفُلَانٍ to such a one. (TA, passim.)
تِلْمِيذٌ A disciple; a pupil; a learner: or a
special servant of a teacher: so says 'Abd-El-
Kádir El-Baghdádee, who composed a treatise
solely on this word: (MF, TA:) or simply a
servant; a follower; a dependant: pl. تَلَامِيذُ
(L, TA) [and تَلَامِذَةٌ].
