تشرينتِشْرِينُ [in Chaldee 165] A Greek name of
each of two months, (K,) of the months of Autumn, called تِشْرِينُ الأَوَّلُ and تِشْرِينُ الثَّانِى, [and
both together تِشْرِينَانِ, the two Syrian months
corresponding, respectively, to October and November O. S.,] before the two months whereof
each is called كَانُونُ. (TA.)
