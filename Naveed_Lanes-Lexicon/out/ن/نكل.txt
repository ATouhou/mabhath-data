نَكِلَ
, aor. يَنْكُلُ, see 1 in art. فضل.
النِّكْلُ
signifies أَلَّذِى يُنْكِلُ أَقْرَانَهُ. (A, art.
عض.) ―
-b2-  See نَكَلٌ.
نَكَلٌ
and ↓ نِكْلٌ: see بَدَلٌ.
نَكَالٌ
Any punishment serving to give warning
to others than the sufferer: (M, K, TA:) or
that restrains the offender from repeating the
offence. (Bd, ii. 6.)
