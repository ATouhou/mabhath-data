نضل1
نَضَلَهُ
He overcame him, or surpassed him,
in shooting. (S, Msb, K.)
3
نَاضَلَهُ
He vied, competed, or contended for
superiority, with him in shooting. (S, Msb, K.)
―
-b2-  نَاضَلَهُ, inf. n. تِنْصَالٌ: see بَيَّنَهَ. ―
-b3-  نَاضَل عَنْهُ
(tropical:)  He defended him, pleaded in defence of him,
or repelled from him; (K, TA;) spoke in his
defence, excusing him; (S, TA;) defended him,
&c., as above; (S;) contended, or pleaded, in
his defence; (TA;) defended him; and contended in his defence. (Msb.)
