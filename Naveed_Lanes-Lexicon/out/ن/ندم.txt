ندم1
نَدِمَ عَلَى مَا فَعَلَ
He grieved for what he
had done; regretted it; repented of it: or he
disliked it. (Msb.)
نَدِيمٌ
A companion in drinking; a cup-companion. (S, Msb, K.) And hence, Any convivial companion; a boon-companion.
مَنْدَمٌ
Repentance: an inf. n. of نَدِمَ; see an
ex. in a verse of El-Kattál El-Kilábee, cited
voce أَىٌّ.
