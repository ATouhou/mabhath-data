نام1
نَامَتْ رِجْلُهُ
(assumed tropical:)  I. q.
خَدِرَتْ [His leg, or foot,
became benumbed, or torpid]. (TA in art. بسر.)
―
-b2-  نَامَ (assumed tropical:)  It (a woman's anklet) ceased to sound,
by reason of the fulness of the leg: like as one
says [in the contr. case], استيقظ. (TA in art.
يقظ.) ―
-b3-  نَامَ has for its inf. ns. نَوْمٌ and مَنَامٌ.
(Msb.) ―
-b4-  نَامَ الثَّوْبُ
i. q.
رَقَدَ [q. v.] (A, art.
رقد.) ―
-b5-  نَامَتِ السُّوقُ
i. q.
رَقَدَت [q. v.] (Th, in
TA, art. رقد.) ―
-b6-  نَامَ (tropical:)  He lay; as opposed to
he sat and he stood. See نَائِمٌ. ―
-b7-  نَامَ عَنْ حَاجَتِهِ
He cared not for the object of his want, neglected
it. (Msb.)
