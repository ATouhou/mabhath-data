نصلنَصْلٌ
The iron head or blade (Mgh, K) of an
arrow, (S, Mgh, K,) and of a spear, (S, K,) and
of a sword, (S, Mgh, Msb, K,) and of a knife,
(S, Msb,) and the like. (Msb.) ―
-b2-  نَصْلٌ The spun
thread of the spindle: (K:) see سُرْسُورٌ.
لِحْيَةٌ نَاصِلٌ
A very white beard. (See العَنْقَاءُ
المُغْرِبُ, art. غرب.) ―
-b2-  مَا بَلِلْتُ مِنْ فُلاَنٍ بِأَفْوَقَ
نَاصِلٍ: see بَلَّ and أَفْوَقَ in two places.
