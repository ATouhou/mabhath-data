نزكنِزْكٌ
The penis (ذَكَر, S, K, i. e. قَضِيب, TA)
of the ضَبّ (S, K) and of the وَرَل: (K: [in the
CK, والوَركُ is erroneously put for والوَرَلِ:)
accord. to the assertion of the Arabs, (S,) it
(S, K,) the former, (S,) as also the حِرْذَوْن,
(Msb voce حرذون,) has two penes (نِزْكَانِ);
(S, K, TA;) and the female has قُرْنَتَانِ, i. e.
[two wombs] رَحِمَانِ. (TA.)
