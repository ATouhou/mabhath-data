نصأ1
نَصَأَهُ, aor.
نَصَاَ, He took him by the نَاصِيَة [or
forelock]: (K:) from نَاصِيَةٌ: and therefore regarded by some as improperly mentioned in the
K in this art. (MF.) ―
-b2-  نَصَأَ, aor.
نَصَاَ, (S, K,)
inf. n. نَصْءٌ, (TA,) He chid, (a camel, TA, or
she-camel, S); syn. زَجَرَ. (AZ, S, K.) ―
-b3-  نَصَأَ,
(S, K,) inf. n. as above, (TA,) He raised, or
elevated, a thing: (Ks, AA, S, K:) dial. form
of نَصَّ. (S.)
