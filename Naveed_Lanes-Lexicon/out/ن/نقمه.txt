نقمه1
نَقَهَ
He recovered, but not completely, his
health and strength: (TA:) or he became convalescent; or sound, or healthy; at the close of
his disease: (S:) or sound, or healthy, but was
yet weak. (K.) See بَرِئَ.
نَقاَهَةٌ
[Convalescence;] the slight degree of
health that immediately succeeds sickness. (TA,
art. برأ.)
