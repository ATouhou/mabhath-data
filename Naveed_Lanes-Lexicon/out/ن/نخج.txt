نخج1
نَخَجَ الدَّلْوَ, (S, K,) and نَخَجَ بِهَا, (TA,)
aor.
نَخَجَ, inf. n. نَخْجٌ, (K,) He agitated, or moved
about, the bucket; (S, K;) a dial. form of مَخَجَ;
(S;) or, accord. to Yaakoob, its ن is substituted
for م: he moved about the bucket in the well in
order that it might fill. (TA.) ―
