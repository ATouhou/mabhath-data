-b2-  نَجَحَ, aor.
نَجَحَ;

إِنْجَاحٌ; (Msb;) He succeeded; he attained, or
accomplished, his want, or wants, or the thing,
or things, that he wanted. (S, Msb, K.) ―
-b3-

-b3-  [So, أَنْجَحَ

-b4-  أنجح بِهِ He, or it, overcame him.

-b2-  ↓ استنجح
