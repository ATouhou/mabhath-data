شَىْءٌ يَدْسُرُهُ البَحْرُ A thing which the sea drives

دَسَرَتِ السَّفِينَةُ المَآءَ بِصَدْرِهَا means The ship repelled, (TA,) or opposed, (M,) the water with
its prow. (M, TA.) ―

Kur liv. 13. (S, TA.)
دَوْسَرٌ A bulky camel: fem. with ة: (S, K:) a
camel (M) strong and bulky; (M, K;) as also
↓ دَوْسَرِىٌّ (S, * M, K) and ↓ دَوْسَرَانِىٌّ (S, * K) and
↓ دُوَاسِرٌ (M, K) and ↓ دُوَسِرِىٌّ: (L:) fem. دَوْسَرٌ
[like the masc.] and دَوْسَرَةٌ: or دَوْسَرٌ, applied to a
she-camel, signifies large: (M:) and ↓ دَوْسَرِىٌّ a
strong camel: (Fr, TA:) and ↓ دُوَاسِرٌ sharp,
spirited, or vigorous, and strong. (TA.) ―
