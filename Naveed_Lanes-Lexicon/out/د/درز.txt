درزدَرْزٌ [A seam, or a raised seam, of a garment or
piece of cloth;] the elevation that is occasioned
in a garment or piece of cloth when its two edges
are put together in the sewing; (Mgh;) one of
the دُرُوز of a garment or piece of cloth, (S, TA,)
and the like; (TA;) which are well known:
(K:) a Persian word, [originally دَرْزْ,] (S, TA,)
arabicized: (S, K:) or, accord. to some, the nap,
or villous substance, (زِئْبِر,) and lustre, (مَآء,) of a
garment, or piece of cloth. (TA.) ―
-b2-  [Hence,]
بَنَاتُ الدُّرُوزِ (tropical:)  Lice: and nits; syn. صِئْبَانٌ. (S,
K, TA.)
