prepares, or makes fit for use, and softens, or
makes pliable, a hide, or skin, consisting of قَرَظ

in the tan, or tanning-liquor]. (S.) And نَقَعَهُ
فِى الدِّبَاغِ [He soaked it in the tanning-liquor].
