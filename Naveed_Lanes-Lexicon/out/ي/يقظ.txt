يَقَظَةٌ A state of waking, or being awake;
(S, Mgh, K;) as also ↓ يَقْظَةٌ, occurring in the
saying of the Et-Tihámee,
العَيْشُ نَوْمٌ وَالْمَنِيَّةُ يَقْظَةٌ
وَالْمَرْءُ بَيْنَهُمَا خَيَالٌ سَارِى
[Life is a state of sleep, and death is a state of
waking; and man between the two is a night
journeying phantom]; but most hold it to be
used only by poetic license. (TA.)
