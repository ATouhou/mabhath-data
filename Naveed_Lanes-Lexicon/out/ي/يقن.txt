, (S, Msb, K,) and يَقِنَ بِهِ; (Msb;)

يَقِينٌ
is of the measure فَعِيلٌ in the sense of
the measure فَاعِلٌ, signifying Sure, or certain,
and manifest. (Msb.) ―
-b2-  And knowledge, or
certainty; [see ظَنٌّ;] instinctive and inferential. (Msb.) ―
-b3-  عَيْنُ اليَقِينِ: see عَيْنٌ (latter
part). ―
-b4-  الحقُّ اليَقِينُ and حَقُّ اليَقِينِ: see جَامِعٌ;
where the two words are said to have the same
meaning: and Bd and Jel in lvi. 95; in the
latter of which, اليقين in حَقُّ اليَقِينِ is expressly
said to be an epithet: see also Ham, p. 335.
