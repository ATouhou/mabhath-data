ثى2
ثَيَّيْتُ ثَآءٌ حَسَنَةً and
حَسَنًا [I made, or wrote,
a beautiful ث]. (TA in باب الالف الليّنة.)
ثِيَّةٌ The lodging-place, or nightly resting-place,
of sheep or goats [when they have gone away to a
distance in the pasture, or around the tents or
houses]; (IB, K;) a dial. var. of ثَايَةٌ [q. v. in
art. ثوى]. (IB, TA.)
ثَاىٌ: see art ثوى.
ثَاىٌ: see art ثوى.
ثَيَوِىٌّ: see ثَائِىٌّ, in art. ثوأ.
