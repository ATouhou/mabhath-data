ثَآءٌ The name of the letter ث, q. v.; as also ثَا:
pl. [of the former,] ثَآءَاتٌ; and [of the latter,]
أَثْوَآءٌ and أَثْيَآءٌ. (TA in باب الالف الليّنة.)
-A2-  It
also stands for ثَنَآءٌ, and ثَوَابٌ, and the like.
(Idem ubi suprà.)
-A3-  Also The best of anything;
as, for instance, of wheat, and of flesh-meat, and
of sugar. (Kh, TA ubi suprà.)
ثَائِىٌّ and ثَاوِىٌّ rel. ns. of ثَآءٌ and ثَا, the names
of the letter ث; as also ثَيَوِىٌّ. (TA ubi suprà.)
