طسطَسٌّ and ↓ طَسَّةٌ (S, M, K) and ↓ طِسَّةٌ (M, K)
i. q. طَسْتٌ, (S, K,) which is expl. in its proper
place: (TA:) [see art. طست: or] طَسَّةٌ is the
original of طَسْتٌ: (Lth, TA:) the pl. (of طَسٌّ,
M) is أَطْسَاسٌ [a pl. of pauc.] (M) and طُسُوسٌ
(S, M, K) and طَسِيسٌ (M, K) [or rather this is a
quasi-pl. n.] and (of طَسَّةٌ and طِسَّةٌ, M) طِسَاسٌ
and [of طَسَّةٌ also] طَسَّاتٌ, (S, K,) and طِسَّةٌ may
also have for its pl. طِسَسٌ, accord. to rule.
(M.) The dim. [of طَسٌّ] is طُسَيْسٌ; (S in art.
طست;) and [of طَسَّةٌ and طِسَّةٌ,] طُسَيْسَةٌ. (IKt
and Msb in art. طست.)
طَسَّةٌ: see the preceding paragraph.
طِسَّةٌ: see the preceding paragraph.
طِسَاسَةٌ The trade of selling, (M,) or the art of
making, (K,) [the kind of basins called] طُسُوس
[pl. of طَسٌّ]. (M, K. *)
طَسَّاسٌ A seller, (M,) or maker, (K,) of [the
kind of basins called] طُسُوس, (M, K. *)
