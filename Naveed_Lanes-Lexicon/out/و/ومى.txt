مَوَامٍ
Lands wherein is nothing. (A'Obeyd,
TA, in art. بلق.) The pl., when indeterminate,
is thus, not مَوَامِى. See a verse in art. عرى,
conj. 12. [This is a correction of art. موم, to
which this word, and also the verb, belong: for
there is no such root as ومى.]
