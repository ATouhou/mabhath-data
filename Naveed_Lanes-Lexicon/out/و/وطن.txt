He took for himself as a home, or

(S, Msb;) as also ↓ أَوْطَنَ (S, Msb, K) and

وَطَنٌ
The place of abode or residence (S,
Mgh, K) of a man: (S, Mgh:) a man's settled
place of abode; his place of constant residence;
his dwelling; his home. (Msb)
