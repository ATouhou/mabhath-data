وَكَزَهُ, (S, Msb,) aor. يَكِزُهُ, (Msb,) inf. n.
وَكْزٌ, (Msb, K,) He struck, or beat him, (S,
Msb,) [with anything,] as, for ex., with a staff,
or stick: (TA:) or it signifies, (Msb,) or signifies also (S) he struck, or beat, him with his
fist upon his chin: (S, Msb:) or, accord. to
Ks, i. q. لَكَمَهُ; (Msb;) [i. e.] he struck, or beat,
him with his fist. (A, K.) ―
-b2-  He pushed, or
impelled, or repelled, him. (S, Msb, K.) ―
-b3-  He
pierced him (Ks, K, * TK) with a spear. (TK.)
―
-b4-  He goaded him. (TA.) ―
-b5-  He broke his
nose. (T, TA.)
وَكْزَةٌ A blow with the fist. (A.)
وَكَّازٌ One who strikes, or beats, much with his
fist. (A.)
مُتَوَكِّزٌ بأَمْرٍ Standing ready, or prepared, for
a thing, or an affair. (L, art. وكد.)
