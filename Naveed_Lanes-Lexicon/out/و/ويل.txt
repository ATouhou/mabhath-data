ويلوَيْلَةُ
and وَيْلًا لَهُ and وَيْلٌ لَهُ
Woe to him! [See
عَوْلَكَ]. Perdition befall him! Punishment befall
him! (Kull, p. 377.) See voce وَيْبٌ, and voce
وَيْحٌ, and وَيْسٌ.
وَيْهًا
[an imperative verbal noun, which may
be rendered On!] a word of incitement. (S, K.)
One also says to a man, and to a horse, يَا وَيْهَاهْ
[Ho! On!]. (A'Obeyd in TA in art. ايه.) See
أَيَّهَ. ―
-b2-  وَيْهَكَ: see أَيْهَكَ in art. ايه. I have not
found this anywhere but in that art. in the K,
and doubt its correctness.
