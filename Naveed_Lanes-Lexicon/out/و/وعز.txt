اوعز إِلَيْهِ فِى كَذَا, (S, K,) or بِكَذَا, (Mgh,)
inf. n. إِيعَازٌ, (Mgh, TA,) He commanded, ordered, or enjoined, him, respecting such a thing,
syn. تقدّم, (S, Mgh, K,) and أَمَرَ, (Mgh, K,)
أَنْ يَفْعَلَ أَوْ يَتْرُكَ that he should do [it] or not do
[it]; (K;) as also اليه ↓ وعّز, (S, K *) inf .n
تَوْعِيزٌ; (S;) and (sometimes S) اليه ↓ وَعَزَ, (S,
K, *) inf. n. وَعْزٌ; (S;) which are also explained
by قَدَّمَ [signifying the same as تَقَدَّمَ and أَمَرَ]:
or the first and second are correct, accord. to
ISk, but the last (وَعَزَ) is not allowable, accord.
to him, nor is it, as related by AHát, accord. to
As. (TA.)
