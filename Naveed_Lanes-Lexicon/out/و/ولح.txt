ولحوَلِيحَةٌ A [sack of the kind called] غِرَارَة: (S,
L, K:) or such as is called a جُوَالِق: or a
large and wide جوالق: (L:) and a date-basket
of palm-leaves; syn. جُلَّةٌ: (S, L, K:) and a
sack or the like forming one half of a beast's
load, in which are carried perfumes and clothes
(بَزّ) and the like: (L:) n. un. of وَلِيجٌ, pl.
وَلَائِحُ. (S, L, K.)
