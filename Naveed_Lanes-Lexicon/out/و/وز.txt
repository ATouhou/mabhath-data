وزوَزٌّ a dial. var. of إِوَزٌّ, (S, K,) A kind of water-fowl; (S;) [the goose, or geese; and the duck,
or ducks; but generally the former of these
birds;] as also ↓ وَزِّينٌ: (K:) n. un. of the
former, وَزَّةٌ; (Msb, art. وز;) and of the latter,
وَزِّينَةٌ. (Sgh, TA.) See كُرْكىّ.
وَزِّينٌ and وَزِّينَةٌ: see وَزٌّ.
أَرْضٌ مَوَزَّةٌ A land abounding with the birds
called وَزّ; (K;) like مَأْوَزَةٌ from إِوَزٌّ. (TA.)
