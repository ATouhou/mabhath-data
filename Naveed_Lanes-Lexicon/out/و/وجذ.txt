وجذوَجْذٌ A small, or round, hollow, or cavity, in
a mountain, (S, L, K,) in which water collects,
(S,) or which retains water, (L, K,) and in which
it stagnates: (L:) or a pool: (L:) and (some
say, TA) a tank, or cistern: (K:) pl. وِجَاذٌ (S,
L, K) and وِجْذَانٌ. (L, K.)
مَكَانٌ وَجِذٌ A place abounding with وِجَاذ, pl.
of وَجْذٌ. (K.)
