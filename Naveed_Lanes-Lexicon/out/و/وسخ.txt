upon a garment, or the skin, (L,) or some other
thing, (Msb,) in consequence of its being seldom
washed, (L,) or from want of care: (Msb:) pl.
أَوْسَاخٌ. (Msb.) ―
-b2-  لَا تَأْكُلْ أوْسَاخَ النَّاسِ (tropical:)  [Live

وَسِخٌ Dirty, or filthy, [in consequence of being
