تَوَسَّمْتُ فِيهِ الخَبْرَ
i. q.
تَفَرَّسْتُهُ; (S;) [I
discovered, or perceived, in him good, or goodness,
by right opinion formed from its outward signs;]
originally, I knew its real existence in him by its
outward sign. (MF.) See also Har, pp. 30,
46, 76. ―
-b2-  تَوَسَّمَ
He examined deliberately
in order to know the real state or character of
a thing by the external sign thereof. (Bd, xv.
75.) ―
-b3-  He perceived a thing by forming a
correct opinion from its outward signs. (TK.)
سِمَةٌ
A brand, or mark or figure made with a
hot iron, upon an animal. (K.) And i. q.
عَلَامَةٌ [A mark, sign, badge, token, symptom,
&c.]. (Msb.) And The عُلْوَان [or title] of a
book or writing. (TA in art. علو.) See also
سِيمَةٌ and سِيمَى in art. سوم.
