وشبوَشْبٌ [app. meaning Fleshy] is from the expression تَمْرَةٌ وَشْبَةٌ, (in one copy of the K, وَشْبَاءُ,
TA,) A date having a thick لِحَآء [i. e. pulp, or
flesh]. (K.) Of the dial. of El-Yemen. (TA.)
وِشْبٌ sing. of أَوْشَابٌ, (K,) which is the same
as أَوْبَاشٌ (S, K) and أَشْوَابٌ, (TA,) [but see this
last word, in art. شوب,] and signifies, A medley,
or mixed multitude; (K;) or sundry, or separate
sorts of people; (S;) and young men of the
baser sort. (TA.) A term of dispraise only.
(Marg. note in a copy of the S.)
