وبخ2
وبّخهُ, inf. n. تَوْبِيخٌ, He reproved him, or
lamed him, (L, Msb, K,) بِسُوْءِ فِعْلِهِ for his evil
action or conduct: (L:) reproved or blamed
him, or did so severely, or with the utmost
severity; and threatened him: (S, L, K:) reproached or upbraided him. (El-Fárábee, Msb.)
ابّخهُ is a dial. form of the same: (IAar:) or
its hemzeh is a substitute for the و. (ISd.)
وَبْخَةٌ A burning reproof: (L:) as also ومْخَةٌ;
(IAar;) in which the ب is changed into م
because of the nearness of their places of utterance. (AM.)
