وَلِهَ
His reason departed, or he became
bereft of his reason or intellect, in consequence of
grief; (K;) or of joy, or grief; (Msb;) or
of intense grief; (S, Mgh;) or of the loss of
the beloved: (TA:) or he grieved, or sorrowed:
(K:) and he became confounded, or perplexed,
and unable to see his right course, (S, K, TA,)
by reason of intense grief: (S, TA:) and he
feared. (K.) See also أَلِهَ, in two places. ―
