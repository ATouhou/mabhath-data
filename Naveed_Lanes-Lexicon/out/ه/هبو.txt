هبو1
هَباَ الرَّمَادُ
, (JK, TA,) aor. يَهْبُو, (TA,) The
ashes became mixed with dust, and extinguished:
(JK, TA:) and هَباَ الجَمْرُ. (M, art. رمد.)
هُبَةٌ
: see أَهْبَةٌ.
هَبْوَةٌ
Dust rising, or spreading, in the sky like
smoke. (JK.)
هَبَآءٌ
The motes that are seen in the rays of the
sun: (TA:) see an ex. voce غُثَآءٌ.
