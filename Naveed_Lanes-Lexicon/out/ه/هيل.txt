هيل7
اِنْهَالَ
It (sand, &c.) poured down. (S, K.)
―
-b2-  اِنْهَدَمَ الجِدَارُ وَآنْهَالَ [The wall fell in ruins,
or to pieces, or became a ruin, and broke, or
crumbled down]. (K in art. قيض.) So
rendered voce اِنْقَاضَ, art. فيض.
