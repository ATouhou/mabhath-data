sedition, discord, or dissension. (JK, K.) It is
said in a trad., (TA,) لَيْسَ فِى الهَيْشَاتِ قَوَدٌ,

cases of conflict and faction, &c., when the slayer
is unknown. (K, TA.) And هَيْشَات in the

هَوْشَات. (TA.)
