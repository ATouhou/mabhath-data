هَرْوَلَ
He walked quickly: (Msb:) [he
went a kind of trotting pace between a walk and
a run; see رَمَلَ;) هَرْوَلَةٌ is not so quick as
خَبَبٌ
[an amble]; (Msb;) and is between
مَشْىٌ [a walk]
and
عَدْوٌ [a run]: (S, Msb, K:) it is a kind
of
عَدْوٌ: (S:) or quicker than
عَنَقٌ: or a quick
walk. (K.)
