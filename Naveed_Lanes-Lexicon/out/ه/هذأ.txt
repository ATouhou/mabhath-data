هذأ1
هَذَأَ, aor.
هَذَاَ, inf. n. هَذْءٌ, He cut a thing
(As. S, K) [quickly], with a sword, &c. (TA.)
It signifies a quicker act than does هَذَّ. (K.) ―
-b2-
هَذَأَ He destroyed the enemy. (K, TA.) ―
-b3-
هَذَأهُ بِلِسَانِهِ, (K, * TA,) inf. n. هَذْءٌ, (TA,) He
hurt, or offended, [or cut,] him with his tongue;
(TA;) made him to hear what he disliked. (K.)
―
-b4-  هَذَأَتِ الإِبِلُ The camels fell down, [and, app.,
died,] one after another: syn. تَسَاقَطَتْ. (K.)
―
-b5-  هَذِئَ مِنَ البَرْدِ, [aor. ??,] He perished of cold.
(K.) ―
-b6-  هَذَأَ الكَلَامَ He was loquacious, with
error. (TA.)
