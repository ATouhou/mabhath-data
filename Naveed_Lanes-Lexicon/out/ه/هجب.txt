هجب1
هَجَبَ, aor.
هَجُبَ, inf. n. هَجْبٌ, He drove, or
urged along. (K.) ―
-b2-  Also, He beat with a
stick, or staff. (K.) ―
-b3-  Also, هَجَبَ, inf. n. as
above, He was quick, or swift, (K,) in his pace
&c. (TA.) ―
-b4-  This art. is omitted by most of
the lexicographers, because not regarded by them
as of established authority. (TA.)
