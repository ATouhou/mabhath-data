هزلجQ. 1
هَزْلَجَ, inf. n. هَزْلَجَةٌ, He (an ostrich, or
anything,) was quick, or swift. (TA.)
هَزْلَجَةٌ: see 1; and art. هزج.
هَزَلَّجٌ A quick, or swift, he-ostrich. (K.)
هِزْلَاجٌ Quick, or swift: (TA:) a light, or an
agile, (and swift, or quick, TA,) wolf: (S, K:)
pl. هَزَالِجُ and هَزَالِيجُ. (TA.) Accord. to Kr,
it is derived from الهَزَجُ. (TA.) [See هزْمَجَةٌ in
art. هزج.]
