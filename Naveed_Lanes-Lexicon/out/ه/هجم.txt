هجم1
هَجَمُ عَلَيْهِ
He came upon him suddenly, or
at unawares, (Mgh, Msb, K,) or came in to him
without permission, (K,) or without asking permission: (Mgh:) he invaded, assaulted, assailed,
attacked, attempted, or ventured upon, him or it:
he pounced upon him or it.
هَجْمَةٌ
, or, accord. to Kz, هَزِيعٌ, The third of
the five divisions of the night. (TA.) See خُدْرَةٌ,
and يَعْفُورٌ. ―
-b2-  As applied to camels, see عَائِضٌ
and زِياَدَةٌ.
