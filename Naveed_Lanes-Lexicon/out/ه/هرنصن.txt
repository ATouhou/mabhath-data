(so in the CK, and in my MS. copy of the K,)
with kesr, (K,) and the ر quiescent, and with kesr
to the ن, (TA,) is A certain worm, (K,) accord. to
IAar; said by others to be (TA) what is called
the
سُرْفَة [which is very variously described].
