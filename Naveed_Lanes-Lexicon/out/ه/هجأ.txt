(K,) It (his hunger) became appeased, (S, K,)
and departed, or ceased. (K.) ―
-b2-  هَجَأَ He ate
food. (K.) ―
-b3-  هَجَأَ, (K,) inf. n. هَجْءٌ, (TA,)
He filled his belly. (K.) ―
-b4-  هَجَأَ and ↓ اهجأ He
stayed, or restrained, camels (K) or sheep or
goats, (TA,) that they might pasture. (K, TA.)
