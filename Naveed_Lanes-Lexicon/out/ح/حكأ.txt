8
احتكأت العُقْدَةُ The knot became tight, or
firm. (Sh, TA.) And احتكأ العِقْدُ فِى عُنُقِهِ The
necklace became fast upon his neck. (Sh, TA.)
―
-b2-  [Hence,] احتكأ الشَّىْءُ فِى صَدْرِى (assumed tropical:)  The thing
became established in my mind, so that I did
not doubt respecting it: and احتكأ الأَمْرُ فِى نَفْسِى
(assumed tropical:)  The matter became established in my mind.
(TA.) [See also اِحْتَكَى; and see اِحْتَكَّ in the
first paragraph of art. حك.] You say also, سَمِعْتُ
أَحَادِيثَ وَمَا احتكأ فِى صَدْرِى مِنْهَا شَىْءٌ (in the
CK ↓ ما احكأ) (assumed tropical:)  I heard stories, and nothing of
them was unsettled in my mind. (K, * TA.) And
لَوِ احتكأ لِى أَمْرِى لَفَعَلْتُ كَذَا (assumed tropical:)  Had my case
been manifest to me at the first, I had done thus.
(L, TA.)
