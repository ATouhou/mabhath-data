الخِلْقَةِ ↓ حَمْشُ (tropical:)  Slender in make, applied to a

to the small bone of the shank: (Msb:) and so
حَمْشَآءُ [the fem.], and ↓ حَمْشَةٌ, and ↓ حَمِيشَةٌ, applied to the shank (سَاق), and to the fore arm

حُمْشٌ (TA) and حِمَاشٌ, (K,) applied to shanks
