say, هُوَ مَحْفُوفٌ بِخَدَمِهِ [He is encompassed, or
surrounded, by his servants]. (TA.) ―
-b2-  هُمْ قَوْمٌ
مَحْفُوفُونَ: see 1.
مِحَفَّةٌ, with kesr; (S, Sgh, Msb, K;) in the
“ Meshárik ” of 'Iyád said to be [مَحَفَّةٌ,] with
fet-h, (MF,) A vehicle of the kind used for women,
like the هَوْدَج, (S, Msb, K,) except that it has no
قُبَّة [or dome-like, or tent-like, top], (S, K,) which
the هودج has: (S:) or a camel's saddle (رَحْل)
surrounded (يُحَفُّ [with pieces of cloth (see 1)
upon a wooden frame]), upon which a woman
rides: accord. to IDrd, so called because the
[frame of] wood [with the pieces of cloth attached
thereto] surrounds on all sides the sitter upon it.
(TA.)
هَوْدَجٌ مُحَفَّفٌ بِدِيبَاجٍ [A هودج hung round
with silk brocade]. (TA.)
