انحذق It (a rope) was, or became, cut.
(K, TA.) Hence the saying of the poet,
يَكَادُ مِنْهُ نِيَاطُ القَلْبِ بَنْحَذِقُ
[The suspensory of the heart is near to becoming
severed in consequence thereof]. (TA.)
