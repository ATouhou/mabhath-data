(K.) ―
-b2-  Also Short and strong: or broad: (K:)
or short and broad; applied to a man: (Th, TA:)
and thick, coarse, rude, or bulky, (K, TA,) and
short. (TA.) [See حَزَابٍ, in two places.] ―
-b3-  The
cock. (K.) ―
-b4-  Also, and ↓ حُنْزُوبٌ, A flock of
[the kind of birds called] قَطًا: (K:) or the male
of the قطا. (TA.) [See حنزاب in art. حزب.] ―
-b5-

(جَزَرُ البَرِّ [see حنزاب in art. حزب]): (K:) n. un.

the proper place of these two words; [the ن being
