حوأحَآءٌ The name of the letter ح, q. v.; as also
حَا: (S and K and TA in باب الالف اللّينة:)
it is called حا in a case of pause, and حَآءٌ when
made a noun: and when it is not called a letter,
[i. e. when one does not prefix to it the word
حَرْف,] it is [properly] fem.: its dim. is حُيَيَّةٌ,
meaning a ح written small, or indistinct: (Lth,
TA ubi suprà:) and its pl. is أَحْوَآءٌ and أَحْيَآءٌ
and حَاآتٌ. (TA ubi suprà.)
حَائِىٌّ and حَاوِىٌّ and حَوِىٌّ [a mistranscription
for حَيَوِىٌّ, like بَيَوِىٌّ and تَيَوِىٌّ,] rel. ns. of حَآءٌ
and حَا the names of the letter ح. (B, TA ubi
suprà.)
