مَنَعَةٌ
State, and power, of resistance; lit. a
state of might of one's people or party, so that
such as desires to do so will not prevail against
him: [or a state of might in his people or party,
&c.; or a state of might, and power of resistance,
in his people or party:] (Msb:) [resistibility: or
simply resistance:] inaccessibleness, or unapproachableness, of a people; as also ↓ مَنْعَةٌ and ↓ مِنْعَةٌ.
(TA.)
