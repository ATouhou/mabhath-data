ميت1
مَاتَ, aor. يَمِيتُ: see art. موت.
دارى بِمِيتَاءِ دَارِهِ, [as also بِمِيدَاءِ,] My house is
opposite to his house. (L, not in the TA.) [See
also art. اتى.] ―
-b2-  مِيتَاءُ طَرِيقٍ The measure of
the two sides, and the distance, of a road; as
also مِيدَاء. (L, not in the TA.) [See also
art أتى.]
