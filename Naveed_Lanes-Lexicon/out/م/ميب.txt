ميبمَيْبَةٌ A certain remedy, or medicine: (K:)
[an arabicized word] from the Persian مَىْ “ a
beverage,” and بِهْ “ a quince: ” quince-beverage;
diacydonium; a medicine prepared of the juice
of quinces: some of it is raw; and some, not
raw: some is perfumed; and some, not perfumed.
(TA, from the book entitled Má lá yesa'u, &c.)
