مصت1
مَصَتَ, ([aor.
مَصُتَ,] inf. n. مَصْتٌ TA,) Inivit
puellam: [K:) dial. form of مصَدَ. (TA.) ―
-b2-
مَصَتَ النَّاقَةَ He laid hold upon the womb of the
camel, and put in his hand, and extracted the
water [i. e. the semen injected into it]. (M, K.)
[See also مَسَطَ.] ―
-b3-  مَصَتَ المِعَى He squeezed
out what was in the intestine, or gut, with his
fingers. (TA.) ―
-b4-  مَصَتَ It (herbage) purged
cattle; or relaxed them in the bowels. (Marg.
note in a copy of the S.) ―
-b5-  مَصَتَ He squeezed
an ulcer, so as to express the matter. (Marg. note
in a copy of the S) ―
-b6-  مَصَتَ He sucked saliva.
(Marg. note in a copy of the S.)
