محو1
مَحَاهُ
He effaced, erased, rased, obliterated,
or cancelled, it; removed, or did away with, its
impression or trace. (K.) ―
-b2-  مَحَتِ الرِّيحُ
عَنْهُ الأَسْقَامَ وَالدُّنُوبَ
The wind made to pass away, or dispelled, the clouds. (TA.) ―
-b3-  مَحَا الصُّبْحُ اللَّيْلَ
Daybreak dispelled the night. (TA.) ―
-b4-  مَحَااللّٰهُ
عَنْهُ الأَسْقَامَ وَالدُّنُوبَ [God removed from him
diseases and sins; as though He cancelled them].
(Msb in art. عفو.) ―
-b5-  الإِحْسَانُ يَمْحُو الإِسَآءَةَ
Beneficence effaces, obliterates, or cancels, evil
conduct. (TA)
