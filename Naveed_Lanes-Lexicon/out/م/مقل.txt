مُقْلٌ
The Theban palm; palma Thebaïca of
Pococke; the cucifera of Theophrastes. ―
-b2-  Also
The خُوص, or leaves, of the tree thus called: see
نظم ―
-b3-  See also صَمْغٌ.
مَقْلَهٌ
, for جُرْعَةُ مَقْلَةٍ: see 3 in art. صفن.
مُقْلَةٌ
The ball, or globe, or bulb (lit. fat,
شَحْمَة),
of the eye, i. e., the eyeball, which comprises the
white and the black. (Khalk el-Insán of Zj;
and S, Msb, K.)
