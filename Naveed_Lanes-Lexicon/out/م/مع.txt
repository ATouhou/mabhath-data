معمَعَ
[generally thus in all cases] is a word, or
noun, (S, K,) or particle, (K,) denoting concomitance, (S, K,) &c. (K.) It is said to
denote the commencement of concomitance,
though this is not invariably the case. (MF
and TA, voce فِى.) ―
-b2-  جِئْتُ مَعَ العَصْرِ means
عِنْدَ العَصْرِ. (Mughnee.)
