منأ1
مَنَأَ, aor.
مَنَاَ, (S, K,) inf. n. مَنْءٌ, (S,) He
soaked a hide in tanning-liquid, or ooze. (S, K.)
-A2-  مَنَأَهُ He conformed with him in what he did.
(TA.)
مَنِيْئَةٌ A hide in the first stage of tanning:
(AZ, S, K:) it is next called أَفِيقٌ; and then
أَدِيمٌ: (AZ, S:) or a hide as long as it remains in
the tanning-liquid. (TA.) ―
-b2-  A place where hides
are tanned. (As, Ks, S, K.) ―
-b3-  Also, accord.
to some, Tan, or that with which one tans.
(TA.) ―
-b4-  This word is not to be pronounced
without ء, because مَنِيَّةٌ signifies “ death. ” (MF.)
مَمْنَأَةٌ A black land: (K:) also without ء.
(TA.)
