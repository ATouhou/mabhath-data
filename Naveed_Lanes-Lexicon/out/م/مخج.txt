مخج1
مَخَجَ الدَّلْوَ, (aor.
مَخَجَ, K, inf. n. مَخْجٌ, TA)
He drew the bucket, and dashed it in the water,
in order that it might fill: (Lh, S, K:) or
مَخَجَ الدَّلْوَ وَغَيْرَهَا, and مَخَجَ بِهَا, and ↓ تمخّجها,
and تمخّج بِهَا, and ↓ تماخجها, and تماخج بها,
he agitated, or moved about, the bucket, &c.
(TA.) ―
-b2-  مَخَجَ البِئْرَ He agitated the water of
the well violently: and he plied the well with the
large bucket. (TA.) ―
-b3-  [Hence,] مَخَجَ, (aor.
مَخَجَ,
inf. n. مَخْجٌ, TA) Inivit feminam. (As, S, K.)
