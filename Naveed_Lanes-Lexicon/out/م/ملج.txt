مُلُجٌ Sucking kids. (K.)
مَلْجَةٌ A single suck. (Msb.) [See also مَلْحَةٌ.]
مَلْجَانُ A man who sucks the teats of his
camels, (or of his sheep or goats, TA,) by
reason of his avarice; (S, K;) not milking
them lest he should be heard: (TA:) as also
مَصَّانُ. (S.)
مَلِيجٌ A foster-brother; syn. رَضِيعٌ. (K.) ―

إِمْلَاجَةٌ A single act of suckling. (TA.) ―
-b2-  It
is said in a trad., لا تُحَرِّمُ الإِمْلَاجَةُ وَلَا
الإِمْلَاجَتَانِ, (S,) i. e. One act of suckling, or the
giving one such, does not bar [the two parties
from marrying each other], nor do two acts of
suckling, or the giving two sucks, like as complete
suckling does. (TA.)
