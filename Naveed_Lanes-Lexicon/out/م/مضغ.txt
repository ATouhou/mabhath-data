مضغمُضْغَةٌ
A piece, or bit, of flesh (T, S, K), &c.:
(T, K:) or a morsel, or gobbet, of flesh, i. e.
a piece of flesh such as a man puts into his
mouth: (Khálid Ibn-Jembeh, TA:) or as much
as is chewed [at once]: (Msb:) and such as
the heart, and the tongue, of a man: (TA:)
and a fœtus when it has become like a lump of
flesh: see Kur. xxii. 5; and see خَلِيقٌ.
