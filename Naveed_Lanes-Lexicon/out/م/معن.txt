معن4
أَمْعَنَ
He (a horse) went far, (S, Msb, K,)
in his run. (S, Msb.) ―
-b2-  Hence, أَمْعَنَ فِى
الطَّلَبِ
He went very far in search: (Msb:) or
he went far, or to a great or an extraordinary
length, therein. (Mgh.) ―
-b3-  امعن فِى الشَّىْءِ,
(Ham p. 817,) or فِى الأَمْرِ, (MA, K, Har p. 176,)
He went far, (K, Ham, Har,) or deep, or beyond
bounds, (MA,) in, or into, the thing, or affair.
(Ham, &c.) ―
-b4-  أَمْعَنَ لِى بِحَقِّى: see أَذَعْنَ.
المَعْنُ
The drawing of water.
